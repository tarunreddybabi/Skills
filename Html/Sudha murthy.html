SUDHA MURTHY

Sudha murthy born 19 August 1950 an Indian educator, author philanthropist who is chairperson of the Infosys Foundation. Murty was awarded the Padma Shri, the fourth highest civilian award in India, for social work by the Government of India in 2006.125l sm

Awards

  1. 2004: Raja-Lakshmi Award by Sri Raja-Lakshmi Foundation in Chenna
  2. 2006: India's fourth highest civilian award Padma Shri
  3. 2006: She also received the R.K. Narayana's Award for Literature.
  4. 2010: Daana Chintamani Attimabbe Award by Karnataka Government
  5. 2018: Murty received the Crossword Book award in popular (Non-Fiction) category
  6. 2019: IIT Kanpur awarded her Honorary Degree (Honoris Causa) of Doctor of Science.
  7. 2023: Padma Bhushan by the Government of India
  8. 2023: Sahitya Akademi Bal Sahitya Puraskar.
